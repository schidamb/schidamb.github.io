Subramanian Chidambaram

Hi! My friends call me “Subbu.” I am a 4th year Ph.D. candidate working in the C Design Lab at Purdue University. My research interest is focused on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), specifically in developing Augmented and Virtual Reality (AR/VR) technology with applications in Mechanical engineering. In addition, I am interested in the development of AR/VR authoring tools, collaboration with AR/VR, and instructional design for skill transfer with AR and VR interfaces. In the past, I have worked in Computational Fabrication and Graphics. I also collaborate with my colleagues in developing wearable computing technology.

On this page, you can find access to both a video summary and papers of my research, published in conferences such as ACM CHI, CSCW,UIST,Ubicomp and DIS. In addition, you can find more about my philosophy for research on the “About me” page.

I am actively looking for Summer Research Internship for 2022 and full-time research opportunities starting December 2022 (Expected to graduate Fall 2022). So, if you find any of my work interesting and have any leads on possible research opportunities, please, get in touch with me. My contact links are available at the bottom of this page. Appreciate you taking the time to check out my work. Cheers!
